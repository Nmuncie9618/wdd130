Overview

Purpose

My website is to educate people about and let them know how they can get involved in or attend heavy combat events. Whether it be information on how to find local tournaments, to how to get started or even just learning what the heck this is all about. As a big fan of the sport and a programmer I am in a unique position to teach people about something they might not even know existed. I know when I first discovered the sport it was the coolest thing I’d ever seen. So, I’d like to spread the word.

Audience

My intended audience is anyone who might be interested in heavy combat or anyone who is ignorant of the sport. Those who would like to learn of the history or how to get involved.
